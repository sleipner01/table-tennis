The clients goal for the website

The client wants a website to make it easier for them to connect with their members, give information, and notify about upcoming events.

The site should be able to be visited by curious visitors, potential members, and members.


Clients wishes for the site

  • Form to apply for membership- contact the organization
  • Information
    • Meetings
    • Matches
    • Training
    • Other events
  • Member Scoreboard
  • A place to read the ruleset
  • Table tennis fun facts
